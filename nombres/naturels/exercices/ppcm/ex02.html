Montrer que ppcm(a,b,c)=ppcm(ppcm(a,b),c)

solution

Puisque les multiples de a et b sont les multiples de leur PPCM, les multiples de ppcm(a,b) et de c, sont donc les multiples de a, de b et de c.

a,b,c, are nonzero natural integers.
Show that lcm(a,b,c)=lcm(lcm(a,b),c)

hint

Review this .

solution

Since the multiples of a and b are the multiples of their LCM, the multiples of lcm(a,b) and of c, are therefore the multiples of a, of b and c.
Sets coinciding so do their smallest element.
