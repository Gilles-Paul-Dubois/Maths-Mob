Examples:
30

1+1+...+1 (30 terms)

hint

Start with case 1, then 2 by hand with a sheet of paper and a pencil.
Show how the solutions for 3 can be deduced from those for 2.
Generalize to show how the solutions for k+1 follow from those for k.
