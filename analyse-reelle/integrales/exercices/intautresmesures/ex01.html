Calculer la longueur de l'arc de la courbe représentative de la fonction

\( \displaystyle f(x)=\frac{x^{3}}{6}+\frac{1}{2x} \)

entre les points d'abscisse 1 et 2.

aide

En paramétrant par l'abscisse (t=x), la longueur de la courbe y=f(x) entre les points d'abscisse a et b devient

\( \displaystyle \int_{a}^{b}\sqrt{1+f'(x)^{2}}dx \)

solution

La longueur cherchée est donc donnée par l'intégrale

\( \displaystyle \int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{1+\left ( \frac{x^{2}}{2}-\frac{x^{-2}}{2} \right )^{2}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{1+\frac{x^{4}}{4}-\frac{1}{2}+\frac{x^{-4}}{4}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{\frac{x^{4}}{4}+\frac{1}{2}+\frac{x^{-4}}{4}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{\left ( \frac{x^{2}}{2}+\frac{x^{-2}}{2} \right )^{2}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\left ( \frac{x^{2}}{2}+\frac{x^{-2}}{2} \right )dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\left [ \frac{x^{3}}{6}-\frac{x^{-1}}{2} \right ]_{1}^{2} \)

\( \displaystyle =\left ( \frac{8}{6}-\frac{1}{4} \right )-\left ( \frac{1}{6} -\frac{1}{2}\right ) \)

\( \displaystyle =\frac{17}{12} \)

Compute the length of the parametric arc represented by the graph of the function

\( \displaystyle f(x)=\frac{x^{3}}{6}+\frac{1}{2x} \)

between the points with abscissa 1 and 2.

hint

Taking x as a parameter (t=x), the length of the curve y=f(x) between points with abscissa a and b is

\( \displaystyle \int_{a}^{b}\sqrt{1+f'(x)^{2}}dx \)

solution

So the length we want is given by the integral

\( \displaystyle \int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{1+\left ( \frac{x^{2}}{2}-\frac{x^{-2}}{2} \right )^{2}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{1+\frac{x^{4}}{4}-\frac{1}{2}+\frac{x^{-4}}{4}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{\frac{x^{4}}{4}+\frac{1}{2}+\frac{x^{-4}}{4}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\sqrt{\left ( \frac{x^{2}}{2}+\frac{x^{-2}}{2} \right )^{2}}dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\int_{1}^{2}\left ( \frac{x^{2}}{2}+\frac{x^{-2}}{2} \right )dx \)

\( \displaystyle =\left [ \frac{x^{3}}{6}-\frac{x^{-1}}{2} \right ]_{1}^{2} \)

\( \displaystyle =\left ( \frac{8}{6}-\frac{1}{4} \right )-\left ( \frac{1}{6} -\frac{1}{2}\right ) \)

\( \displaystyle =\frac{17}{12} \)
