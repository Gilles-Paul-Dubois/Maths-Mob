M=f(a)

We now claim that:
théorème 1
Dans ce cas précis (f continue et strictement monotone), f-1 est également continue.
theorem 1
In this special case (f continuous and strictly monotone), f-1 is also continuous.

We then see that f is everywhere 'locally invertible' (in the sense of composition) and that its 'local' inverse is continuous, but its 'local' inverse everywhere coincides with its 'global' inverse.

théorème 2
Toute fonction continue f et strictement monotone sur un intervalle (quelconque) possède une réciproque elle aussi strictement monotone (de même variation que f) et également continue.
theorem 2
Any continuous function f and strictly monotonic on any interval has an inverse that is also strictly monotonic (with the same variation as f) and also continuous.
