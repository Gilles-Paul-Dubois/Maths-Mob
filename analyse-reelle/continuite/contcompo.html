Composite of two continuous functions

Review of two numeric functions. The continuity of composite functions follows immediately from the theorems on .
We can therefore state the following result, concerning two numerical functions (f,D), (g,D') such that f(D)⊆D':
propriété 1
Si f est une fonction continue en x0 et si g est continue en y0=f(x0) alors gof est continue en x0.
property 1
If f is a continuous function at x0 and if g is continuous at y0=f(x0) then gof is continuous at x0.
Ce résultat se globalise ainsi:
This result can be made global as follows:
propriété 2
Si f est continue sur D et si g est continue en tout point de f(D) alors gof est continue sur D.
property 2
If f is continuous on D and if g is continuous at every point of f(D) then gof is continuous on D.
