Let (r,u) and (s,v) be two series.
Let λ and μ be two real numbers.
We consider the series (t,w) with general term:
wn=λun+μvn
Show that if r is convergent with sum a and if s is convergent with sum b, then t is convergent with sum c=λa+μb.
Show that if (p,z) is the series with general term:
zn=unvn
p generally does not converge to ab.
Show that it is possible that r and s do not converge but that p converges.

hint

Show that there is between tn, rn and sn the same relation as between wn, un and vn.
