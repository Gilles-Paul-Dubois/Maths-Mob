We consider the recurrent sequence defined by:

  • u0=4
  • un=un-1+4×(-1)n/(2n+1)
This sequence, due to Gregory (and/or) Leibniz, slowly converges to the number π.
See for example Boris Gourévitch's excellent site: .
Boris Gourévitch showed that it could be accelerated significantly by constructing a sequence which is a weighted average of the original sequence.
While Bromberg and Aitken offer weighted averages of two consecutive terms, he offers a 4-term average.
That is, he builds the following:
mn=(un+3un-1+3un-2+un-3)/8
and he makes us note that the acceleration obtained is better than that obtained by Aitken's process.
Write a python and/or julia program that calculates the difference between:
  • π and un
  • π and vn (Aitken acceleration of u)
  • π and mn
to order 998.
