Sequences of real numbers

Index of pages

This constitutes the first chapter of calculus (real analysis).
It is particularly important because it introduces for the first time the notion of 'limit' .
This notion is at the very heart of the theory.
It is important to be familiar with the definitions and the main theorems.
All this is then transported without problem to the functions of a real variable.
We will be able to convince ourselves, from this first chapter, that 'doing calculus' is essentially 'solving inequalities' .
