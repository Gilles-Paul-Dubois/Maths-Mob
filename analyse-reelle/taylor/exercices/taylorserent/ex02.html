solution

Si f et g sont développables en séries entières au voisinage de x0, on a :

\( \displaystyle f(x) = {\sum\limits_{k = 0}^{\infty}a_{n}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n}} \)

pour |x-x0| < R1 où R1 est le rayon de convergence de la série entière de coefficients an. Et on a aussi :

\( \displaystyle g(x) = {\sum\limits_{k = 0}^{\infty}b_{n}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n}} \)

pour |x-x0| < R2 où R2 est le rayon de convergence de la série entière de coefficients bn.
Donc si R3=Inf(R1,R2), on a :

\( \displaystyle \left( {f + g} \right)(x) = {\sum\limits_{n = 0}^{\infty}\left( a_{n} + b_{n} \right)}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n} \)

pour |x-x0| < R3.
On a en outre :

\( \displaystyle ({\lambda f})(x) = {\sum\limits_{n = 0}^{\infty}\left( \lambda a_{n} \right)}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n} \)

Ce qui établit la structure d'espace vectoriel.
La structure d'algèbre provient de ce que :

\( \displaystyle ({f\times g})(x) = {\sum\limits_{n = 0}^{\infty}c_{n}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n}} \)

où

\( \displaystyle c_{n} = {\sum\limits_{k + h = n}a_{h}b_{k}} \)

(théorème sur les produits de convolution des séries entières).

solution

If f and g are expandable into integer series in the neighborhood of x0, we have:

\( \displaystyle f(x) = {\sum\limits_{k = 0}^{\infty}a_{n}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n}} \)

for |x-x0| < R1 where R1 is the radius of convergence of the entire series of coefficients an. And we also have:

\( \displaystyle g(x) = {\sum\limits_{k = 0}^{\infty}b_{n}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n}} \)

for |x-x0| < R2 where R2 is the radius of convergence of the entire series of coefficients bn.
So if R3=Inf(R1,R2), we have:

\( \displaystyle \left( {f + g} \right)(x) = {\sum\limits_{n = 0}^{\infty}\left( a_{n} + b_{n} \right)}\left ( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n} \)

for |x-x0| < R3.
We also have:

\( \displaystyle ({\lambda f})(x) = {\sum\limits_{n = 0}^{\infty}\left( \lambda a_{n} \right)}\left( {x - x_{0 }} \right)^{n} \)

Which establishes the vector space structure.
The algebra structure comes from the fact that:

\( \displaystyle ({f\times g})(x) = {\sum\limits_{n = 0}^{\infty}c_{n}\left( {x - x_{0}} \right)^{n }} \)

where

\( \displaystyle c_{n} = {\sum\limits_{k + h = n}a_{h}b_{k}} \)

(theorem on convolution products of power series).
