In the same way that it was a matter of establishing criteria of comparison for the growth of sequences, we can extend Landau's notations to numerical functions. The only difference being that in the case of sequences, the integer variable can only tend towards +∞ although that in the case of numerical functions, there are for the real variable many types of possible behaviors, just to remind you :
  • x→+∞
  • x→-∞
  • x→x0
  • x→x0 x≠x0
  • x→x0+
  • x→x0+ x≠x0
  • x→x0-
  • x→x0- x≠x0
In order not to multiply analogous statements, we will once again use the notation x→... where ... can designate any of the 8 preceding cases.
