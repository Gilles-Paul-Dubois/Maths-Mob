solution

It is clear that fn(x) simply converges to x ∀ x ≥ 0.
The fact that the convergence is uniform on [0,1] results from the , because it is a decreasing monotonic sequence converging to a continuous function on a compact interval.

\( \displaystyle f_{n}'(x)=\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^{2}+\frac{1}{n^{2}}}} \)

Let us now set xn=1/n ∀ n ≥ 1.
We have fn'(xn)=1/√2.
As a result, fn' does not converge uniformly to 1 over [0,1].
