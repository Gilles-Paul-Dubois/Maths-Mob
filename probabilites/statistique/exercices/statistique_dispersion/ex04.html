Use a spreadsheet to determine:

  1. the range
  2. the mean absolute deviation
  3. the variance
  4. the standard deviation
of a statistical series.

hint

Use the function wizard and choose statistical functions.
There are several functions' VAR' and several 'STDEV' functions (choose the correct ones using the online help).
There is no 'RANGE' function (use MAX and MIN instead).
