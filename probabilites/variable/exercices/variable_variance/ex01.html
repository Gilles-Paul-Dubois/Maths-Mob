Calculer la variance d'une .

aide

On sait qu'une telle variable a pour espérance λ.
Utilisez X2=X(X-1)+X.

solution

\( \displaystyle v(X) = E\left( X^{2} \right) - {E(X)}^{2} \)

\( \displaystyle v(X) = E(X(X - 1)) + \lambda - \lambda^{2} \)

\( \displaystyle E(X(X - 1)) = {\sum\limits_{k = 0}^{\infty}\frac{k(k - 1)\lambda^{k}e^{- \lambda}}{k!}} \)

\( \displaystyle E(X(X - 1)) = {\sum\limits_{k = 2}^{\infty}\frac{k(k - 1)\lambda^{k}e^{- \lambda}}{k!}} \)

\( \displaystyle E(X(X - 1)) = e^{- \lambda}{\sum\limits_{k = 2}^{\infty}\frac{\lambda^{2}\lambda^{k - 2}}{(k - 2)!}} \)

\( \displaystyle E(X(X - 1)) = e^{- \lambda}{\sum\limits_{k = 0}^{\infty}\frac{\lambda^{2}\lambda^{k}}{k!}} = \lambda^{2} \)

d'où   \( \displaystyle v(X) = \lambda \)
