On suppose que l'on a cinq urnes.

  1. Deux urnes, n° 1 et n°2, contiennent 2 boules blanches et 3 boules noires (composition A1).
  2. Deux urnes, n° 3 et n° 4,contiennent 1 boule blanche et 4 noires (composition A2).
  3. Une urne, n°5, contient 4 boules blanches et 1 boule noire (composition A3).
De l'une de ces urnes (choisie au hasard) on extrait une boule.
B désigne l'évènement : "la boule est blanche".
Sachant que la boule tirée est blanche, quelle est la probabilité qu'elle soit issue de l'urne n°5 ?
Effectuer une simulation sur ordinateur.

aide

Appliquer le théorème de Bayes.

solution

Traduction des hypothèses :
P(A1)=2/5
P(A2)=2/5
P(A3)=1/5
P(B|A1)=2/5
P(B|A2)=1/5
P(B|A3)=4/5
La formule de Bayes donne :

\( \displaystyle P\left( A_{3} \mid B \right) = \frac{P\left( A_{3} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{3}} \right)}{P\left( A_{1} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{1}} \right) + P\left( A_{2} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{2}} \right) + P\left( A_{3} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{3}} \right)} = \frac{\frac{1}{5} \times \frac{4}{5}}{\frac{2}{5} \times \frac{2}{5} + \frac{2}{5} \times \frac{1}{5} + \frac{1}{5} \times \frac{4}{5}} = \frac{2}{5} \)

We assume that we have five urns.

  1. Two urns, No. 1 and No. 2, contain 2 white balls and 3 black balls (composition A1).
  2. Two urns, No. 3 and No. 4, contain 1 white ball and 4 black ones (composition A2).
  3. An urn, n°5, contains 4 white balls and 1 black ball (composition A3).
From one of these urns (chosen at random) we extract a ball.
B designates the event: "the ball is white".
Knowing that the ball drawn is white, what is the probability that it comes from urn n°5?
Perform a computer simulation.

hint

Apply Bayes' theorem.

P(A2)=2/5
P(A3)=1/5
P(B|A1)=2/5
P(B|A2)=1/5
P(B|A3)=4/5
Bayes' formula gives:

\( \displaystyle P\left( A_{3} \mid B \right) = \frac{P\left( A_{3} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{3}} \right)}{P \left( A_{1} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{1}} \right) + P\left( A_{2} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{2} } \right) + P\left( A_{3} \right)P\left( {B \mid A_{3}} \right)} = \frac{\frac{1}{5} \times \frac{ 4}{5}}{\frac{2}{5} \times \frac{2}{5} + \frac{2}{5} \times \frac{1}{5} + \frac{1} {5} \times \frac{4}{5}} = \frac{2}{5} \)
