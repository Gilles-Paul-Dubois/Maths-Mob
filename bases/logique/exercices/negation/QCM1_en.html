NEGATION OF A PROPOSITION

Choose the correct negation for the given proposition !

1. There's life on Mars.

There are a few evidences of life on Mars.

There cannot be any life on Mars.

The probability of life on Mars is very low.

There's no life on Mars.

2. All basketball players are tall.

Some basketball players are tall.

Some basketball players are not tall.

All basketball players are small.

Some basketball players are very small.

3. My sister has a few recent books.

My sister didn't buy any new book.

My sister has only old books.

My sister has an old book.

My sister has no old books.
