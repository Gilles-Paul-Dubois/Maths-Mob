hint

Determine the first integer not equal to its image, then isolate a first cycle.
Start again with the first integer not equal to its image and not belonging to the previous cycle, and so on.
Break down each cycle into transpositions.
Deduce the signature from the decomposition into transpositions.
Find the order of p from its decomposition into cycles.

solution

The decomposition is :
(9,0) transposition
(4,7,8,2) cycle of length 4
(3 5 6 1) cycle of length 4
(4,7,8,2) breaks down into en 3 transpositions
(3 5 6 1) also
So p is a factor of 7 transpositions
The signature is thus -1
Order is 4 which is the least common multiple of orders of the three cycles entering the decomposition.
