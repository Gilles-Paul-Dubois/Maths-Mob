Soient E,F,G 3 ensembles
On suppose que
f: E → F
g: F → G
sont deux bijections
Démontrer que gof est aussi une bijection et que:
(gof)-1=f-1 og-1

aide

Etudier la composée de deux  injections, de deux surjections.
Calculer gof(f-1 og-1(x))

solution

La composée de deux injections est une injection.
La composée de deux surjections est une surjection.
La composée de deux bijections est donc une bijection.
gof(f-1 og-1(x))=g(f(f-1(g-1(x))))=g(g-1(x))=x
Ce qui prouve la seconde assertion.

E,F,G are 3 sets
We suppose that
f: E → F
g: F → G
are two bijections
Show that gof is as well a bijection and that :
(gof)-1=f-1 og-1

hint

Study the composite of two  injections, of two surjections.
Compute gof(f-1 og-1(x))

solution

The composite of two injections is again an injection.
The composite of two surjections is again a surjection.
The composite of two bijections is therefore a bijection.
by associativity :
gof(f-1 og-1(x))=g(f(f-1(g-1(x))))=g(g-1(x))=x
Which proves our second assertion.
