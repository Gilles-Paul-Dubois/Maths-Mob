Show that with the binary operation * defined on ℝ -{1} by : x * y= x+y-xy
ℝ -{1} becomes a commutative group.

hint

Review these 3 exercises

solution

Since ℝ -{1} is stable by the operation (x,y) → x*y sur ℝ ,  * is a restriction to ℝ -{1} of the same operation on ℝ
We have seen that this operation is associative and commutative, with neutral 0.
