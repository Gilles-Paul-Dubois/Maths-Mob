Ring and field isoomorphisms should be isomorphisms for both operations together. So the first example, although being an automorphism of (A,+) won't be, in general a ring automorphism of (A,+, ×) because only in a few special cases we will have a(xy)=(ax)(ay) a idempotent and A commutative.
