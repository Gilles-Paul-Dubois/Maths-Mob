  2. m1m2=m3. The composite of the two axial symmetries is the symmetry relative to the third axis.
  3. m1M1=-Id The composite of the two symmetries is therefore a central symmetry (homotety) with ration -1.
  4. M1M2=m3. The composite of the two plane symmetries is therefore an axial symmetry elative to their intersection.
