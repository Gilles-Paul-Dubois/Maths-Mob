Soit ABCD un parallélogramme.
Soient E,F,G,H les milieux de [AB],[BC],[CD],[DA] respectivement.

Montrer que EFGH est également un parallélogramme.

aide

Calculer \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{EF} \) et \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{HG} \) en fonction de \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{AB} \) et \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{BC} \) .

solution

Par Chasles \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{EF}=\overrightarrow{EB}+\overrightarrow{BF} \) et \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{HG}=\overrightarrow{HD}+\overrightarrow{DG} \) .
Mais \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{DG}=\overrightarrow{EB}=\frac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{AB} \) et \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{BF}=\overrightarrow{HD}=\frac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{BC} \) .

Let ABCD be a parallelogram.
Let E,F,G,H be the midpoints of [AB],[BC],[CD],[DA] respectively.

Show that EFGH is also a parallelogram.

hint

Calculate \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{EF} \) and \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{HG} \) depending on \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{AB} \) and \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{BC} \) .

solution

By Chasles \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{EF}=\overrightarrow{EB}+\overrightarrow{BF} \) and \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{HG}=\overrightarrow{HD}+\overrightarrow{DG} \) .
But \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{DG}=\overrightarrow{EB}=\frac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{AB} \) and \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{BF}=\overrightarrow{HD}=\frac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{BC} \) .
