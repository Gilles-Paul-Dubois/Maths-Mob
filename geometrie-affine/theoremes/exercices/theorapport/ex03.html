Q divise [PP'] dans le rapport λ.
Q' divise [PP'] dans le rapport λ'.
Calculer les rapports de partage :
\( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{PQ}}{\overline{PQ'}} \) et \( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{P'Q}}{\overline{P'Q'}} \)

aide

Traiter le problème avec un axe d'origine P.

solution

Sur un axe d'origine P les abscisses des points sont :
P(0) P'(a) Q(λa/(1-λ)) Q'(λ'a/(λ'-1))
On trouve donc :
\( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{P'Q}}{\overline{P'Q'}}=\frac{\lambda '-1}{\lambda -1} \)
et
\( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{PQ}}{\overline{PQ'}}=\frac{\lambda \left (\lambda '-1 \right )}{\lambda '\left (\lambda -1 \right )} \)

Q divides [PP'] with ratio λ.
Q' divides [PP'] with ratio λ'.
Calculate split (sharing)) ratios:
\( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{PQ}}{\overline{PQ'}} \) and \( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{P'Q}}{\overline{P'Q'}} \)

hint

Treat the problem with an axis with P as origin.

solution

On an axis of origin P, the abscissas of the points are:
P(0) P'(a) Q(λa/(1-λ)) Q'(λ'a/(λ'-1))
We therefore find:
\( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{P'Q}}{\overline{P'Q'}}=\frac{\lambda '-1}{\lambda -1} \)
and
\( \displaystyle \frac{\overline{PQ}}{\overline{PQ'}}=\frac{\lambda \left (\lambda '-1 \right )}{\lambda '\left (\lambda -1 \right )} \)
