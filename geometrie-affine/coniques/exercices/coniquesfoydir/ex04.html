aide

Inverser les x et les y et calculer c.

solution

On a immédiatement a=3 d'où d=9/c et c= √13.

Equation of the hyperbola centered at the origin with vertex S(0,-3) and directrix y=9/√13.

hint

Invert the x's and y's and calculate c.

solution

We immediately have a=3, hence d=9/c and c= √13.
It follows that b=2.
If the focal axis was x'Ox the equation would be:

\( \displaystyle \frac{x^{2}}{9} - \frac{y^{2}}{4} = 1 \)

The equation is therefore:

\( \displaystyle \frac{y^{2}}{9} - \frac{x^{2}}{4} = 1 \)
