On considère la figure suivante :

formée par deux droites parallèles xOy et x'O'y' et une sécante commune zOO'z'.
Démontrer toutes les égalités d'angles suggérées par l'emploi de couleurs et de notations identiques.

aide

Trouver des déplacements faisant entrer les angles en correspondance.

solution

  \( \displaystyle \widehat{xOz}=\widehat{yOz'} \)   ( )
  \( \displaystyle \widehat{zOy}=\widehat{z'Ox} \)   (idem)
  \( \displaystyle \widehat{xOz}=\widehat{x'O'z} \)   (translation)
  \( \displaystyle \widehat{zOy}=\widehat{zO'y'} \)   (idem)

Des angles tels que   \( \displaystyle \widehat{xOz} \)   et   \( \displaystyle \widehat{x'O'z'} \)   sont appelés 'correspondants' .

Des angles tels que   \( \displaystyle \widehat{yOz'} \)   et   \( \displaystyle \widehat{x'O'z} \)   sont appelés 'alternes internes' .

Des angles tels que   \( \displaystyle \widehat{zOy} \)   et   \( \displaystyle \widehat{z'O'x'} \)  sont appelés 'alternes externes' .

We consider the following figure:

formed by two parallel lines xOy and x'O'y' and a common secant zOO'z'.
Prove all the equalities of angles suggested by the use of identical colors and notations.

hint

Find proper isometries that match the angles.

solution

  \( \displaystyle \widehat{xOz}=\widehat{yOz'} \)   ( )
  \( \displaystyle \widehat{zOy}=\widehat{z'Ox} \)   (same)
  \( \displaystyle \widehat{xOz}=\widehat{x'O'z} \)   (translation)
  \( \displaystyle \widehat{zOy}=\widehat{zO'y'} \)   (same)

Angles such as   \( \displaystyle \widehat{xOz} \)   And   \( \displaystyle \widehat{x'O'z'} \)   are called 'matching' .

Angles such as   \( \displaystyle \widehat{yOz'} \)   And   \( \displaystyle \widehat{x'O'z} \)   are called 'internal alternates' .

Angles such as   \( \displaystyle \widehat{zOy} \)   And   \( \displaystyle \widehat{z'O'x'} \)  are called 'external alternates' .
