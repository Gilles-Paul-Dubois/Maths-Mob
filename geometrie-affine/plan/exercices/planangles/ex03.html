Show that α+β=ϖ.

hint

solution


  \( \displaystyle \widehat{DAB} \)   And   \( \displaystyle \widehat{CBE} \)   are equal because 'corresponding' for the 2 lines // (AD) and (BC) and the secant (AB).
  \( \displaystyle \widehat{BCD} \)   And   \( \displaystyle \widehat{CBE} \)   are equal because 'internal alternates' for the 2 lines // (AB) and (DC) and the secant (CB).
