Let T be the translation of vector   \( \displaystyle \overrightarrow{u} \)  .
Let R be the rotation with center O and angle α.
Construct geometrically the center Ω of R'=ToR.

hint

Use .

solution

Construct the translate of O by T that is O'.
We have O'=R'(O) since O is fixed by R.
Let O" be the image of O by the rotation of angle α .
Let O"'=T(O") so that O"' is the image of O' by R'.
The Ω is at the intersection of the perpendicular bisector of [OO'] with the perpendicular bisector of [O'O"']
